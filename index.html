Who We Are

We are a team of experts in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.

We strive for opportunities to work on solutions to real world problems using state-of-the-art artificial intelligence techniques. We believe in working with honesty and integrity to provide the best service we can. Our team specializes in the following fields.

Machine Learning

We have worked with a wide range of techniques starting from the traditional ML techniques such as Stemming, NER, Markov models etc, to the more modern deep learning techniques such as convolutional and recurrent neural networks. We have used TensorFlow, Keras, Scikit Learn in Python.

Computer Vision

We are experts in utilizing Machine Learning & Deep Neural Network Models to perform the tasks of Object Detection, Classification, Segmentation & Masking. We have experience in OpenCV library in Python.

Natural Language Processing

We have experience with various Macro Understanding techniques like Word2Vec, Dimensionality Reduction, Nearest Neighbor, Classification as well as Clustering Algorithms. We are well versed with nltk library in Python.

Data Science & Visualization

We use Matplotlib, Seaborn, Pygal, Facets & Ggplot to visualize and derive meaningful conclusions from the data.

What We Do

We harness the potential of Industry 4.0 Artificial Intelligence Techniques to solve industry level problems at scale.

We also help organizations integrate Machine Learning into their products to achieve better efficiency, higher performance and effective cost cutting.

Recommendation Systems

Ranging from movie recommendations to product recommendations, from music genre recommendation to candidate hiring recommendation, whatever you want to recommend to your customers, we are here to build automated recommendation engines for you.

Real-time Predictive Analysis

We apply predictive analytics to optimize marketing campaigns to determine customer behavior and improve operations to increase work efficiency and reduce costs. We also specialize in sentiment analysis, social media behaviour analysis and sales forecasting.

Pattern Recognition

We are capable of extracting patterns and inferences from large amounts of data using Deep Learning Algorithms to help organizations make better decisions. We specialize in speech recognition and image classification problems.

Chatbots/Virtual Assistants

We use natural language processing techniques and sequence-to-sequence modelling to build robust chatbots and virtual assistants that can enhance the user experience of your product/website.

Fraud Detection

We apply Artificial Intelligence techniques to perform Anomaly Detection, Spam and Malware Classification and other types of fraud detection areas to help you safeguard your business from all kinds of fraudulent activities.

Automated Surveillance Systems

We can build intelligent security systems which perform real-time automated surveillance on CCTV footage to detect abnormal behaviours and send alerts.
